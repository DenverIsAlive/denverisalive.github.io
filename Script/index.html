The Setup is Complete, & The Script is Ready to Use.

How to Login:

  • Open the Login Page (port 80 for XAMPP): http://localhost:8000/TP/login.php
  • You can Also Use default IP URL: http://127.0.0.1:8000/TP/login.php
  • Or Your Wifi IP URL which is shown in KSweb Home Page top (use port 8000 for mobile & TV, 80 for PC).
  • Login with Your TP Sub ID & enter the OTP received on Your RMN to login.

Note: The Account You Use Must be Active.

How to Access M3U Playlist:

In Tivimate, NS Player & OTT Navigator, use the following Playlist link (port 80 for XAMPP):

http://localhost:8000/tataplay/Playlist.m3u

You can also copy the Playlist URL from index.php. Now Enjoy Your Tataplay Channels 🎉.
